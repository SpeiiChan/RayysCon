OUR CHARACTERS DESIGN

01

GENDO

Cold, manipulative commander of NERV. Obsessed with reuniting with his deceased wife Yui. He uses his son Shinji and others as tools for his Human Instrumentality Project.

02

SHINJI

The reluctant protagonist and pilot of Eva Unit-01. Struggles with loneliness, self-worth, and his strained relationship with Gendo, embodying the series' themes of depression and human connection.

03

REI

Mysterious First Child and pilot of Unit-00. A clone derived from Yui Ikari and Lilith, she is quiet, enigmatic, and central to Gendo's plans.

04

ASUKA

Fiery Second Child and pilot of Unit-02. Proud and competitive, she masks deep trauma and insecurity beneath her bravado, representing the struggle for recognition and love.

05

MARI

Introduced in the Rebuild films, she is playful, confident, and enjoys piloting Evas. Less burdened by trauma than others, she brings levity and unpredictability to the team.

06

MISATO

Operations director at NERV. Charismatic and caring toward Shinji and Asuka, yet burdened by her past and complex relationship with Ryoji Kaji. She balances maternal warmth with military leadership.
